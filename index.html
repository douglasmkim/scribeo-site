Privacy Policy

Scribeo — Last updated May 20, 2026

Scribeo is designed with privacy at its core. Your recordings, transcriptions, and notes never leave your device.

Data We Collect

None. Scribeo does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Audio & Microphone

Scribeo requests microphone access solely to record audio that you choose to capture. Audio recordings are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared with any third party.

On-Device Processing

All transcription and summarization is performed entirely on your device using local machine-learning models. No audio, text, or note content is sent to any server for processing.

Network Usage

The only network request Scribeo makes is to download an optional on-device summarization model. This download does not transmit any personal or usage data.

Data Storage

All your data — recordings, transcriptions, and notes — is stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in data frameworks. You can delete your data at any time by removing notes within the app or by deleting the app entirely.

Third-Party Services

Scribeo does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Scribeo does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.
