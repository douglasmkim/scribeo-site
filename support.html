Frequently Asked Questions

How does transcription work?

Scribeo uses on-device AI to transcribe your recordings. Everything is processed locally on your iPhone — no audio is ever uploaded to a server.

What languages are supported?

Scribeo supports over 100 languages with automatic language detection. You can also manually select a language in settings.

How do I generate a summary?

After a recording is transcribed, tap the summarize button to generate structured notes from the transcript. Summarization also runs entirely on your device.

Can I import audio files?

Yes. You can import audio files from the Files app or other apps using the share sheet.

Where is my data stored?

All recordings, transcriptions, and notes are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded or synced to any server.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual notes and their audio from within the app. To remove all data, delete the app from your device.
